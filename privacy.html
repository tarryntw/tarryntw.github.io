Privacy Statement

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • name
  • contact information
  • location
  • interactions with us
  • billing or purchase information

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • Create bookings with our Restaurant

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter Personal information, we'll book a table for you.

We keep your information safe by Storing it with in our encrypted database and only allowing booking staff to access it .

We keep your information for two years at which point we securely destroy it by automatically removing the data from our systems.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at Admin@haukai.com.
